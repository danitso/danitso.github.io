Welcome to Danitso!

Your shortcut to practical DevOps

We provide a wide range of DevOps components, including Docker images, Terraform providers and CLI utilities. The components are meant to ease the transition from traditional on-premise solutions to modern cloud solutions powered by Infrastructure as Code.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between Application Developers and System Administrators, and thereby fully embrace the DevOps concept. We want the Application Developers to be able to choose the best infrastructure for their applications, without them having to understand how all of these complex solutions are configured. We know from experience that abstraction layers are a good way to achieve this type of synergy, which is why we design our solutions with that in mind.
